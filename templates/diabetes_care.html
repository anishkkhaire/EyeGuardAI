Diabetes Care Guide

Managing diabetes effectively is key to preventing complications like diabetic retinopathy. Follow these scientifically-backed wellness tips to achieve better health and protect your vision.

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Include fiber, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Avoid processed foods and excessive sugar.

2. Exercise Daily

Walk, do yoga, or strength train for at least 30 minutes daily to improve insulin sensitivity.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drink 2-3 liters of water daily to help flush out excess glucose from the body.

4. Get Enough Sleep

7-8 hours of sleep daily helps control blood sugar levels and prevents insulin resistance.

5. Monitor Blood Sugar Levels

Check your blood sugar regularly and adjust your diet & medications accordingly.

6. Manage Stress

Practice meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises to control stress and blood sugar.

Afternoon: Balanced lunch, hydration, mindful eating.
Evening: Light dinner, walk, proper sleep routine.
