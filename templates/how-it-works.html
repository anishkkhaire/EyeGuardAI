How EyeGuard AI Works

Follow these simple steps to detect diabetic retinopathy using AI.

1. Upload Retinal Image

Patients securely upload their high-resolution retinal images.

2. AI Analysis

Our AI model analyzes the image to detect diabetic retinopathy.

3. Doctor Review

A licensed ophthalmologist verifies the AI's diagnosis and provides insights.

4. Receive Diagnosis Report

Patients receive a report with AI predictions and doctor recommendations.
